About SafeIndy

Empowering Indianapolis residents with AI-powered public safety assistance

Our Mission

SafeIndy Assistant bridges the gap between Indianapolis residents and public safety resources through intelligent, real-time assistance powered by artificial intelligence.

We believe every resident deserves quick, accurate access to emergency services, city resources, and community safety information. Our AI-powered platform provides 24/7 assistance, helping you navigate safety concerns, report issues, and access the help you need when you need it most.

Key Features

24/7 AI Chat Support

Get instant responses to safety questions and service requests any time of day.

Real-time Local Data

Access current Indianapolis city services, alerts, and community information.

Emergency Routing

Direct connection to 911 and non-emergency services when you need them most.

Community Reports

Anonymous reporting system for neighborhood safety concerns and updates.

How It Works

1
Ask Your Question

Technology Stack

AI & Machine Learning
  • Groq LLM for conversational AI
  • Perplexity Sonar for real-time data
  • RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation)
  • Qdrant vector database
Data Sources
  • Indianapolis.gov live data
  • Mayor's Action Center API
  • Google Maps integration
  • Real-time weather alerts

Privacy & Security

Data Protection
  • No personal data stored permanently
  • Session-based conversations only
  • Anonymous community reporting
  • Encrypted data transmission
Emergency Priority
  • Direct 911 routing for emergencies
  • No delays for critical situations
  • Immediate emergency contact display
  • Clear emergency vs. non-emergency guidance

Community Impact

SafeIndy Assistant was built for the Indianapolis Civic Tech Hackathon 2025 with the goal of serving all 870,000+ Indianapolis residents. Our focus is on:

  • Accessibility: Available 24/7 in multiple languages
  • Equity: Free access to all residents
  • Efficiency: Faster response times for service requests
  • Transparency: Clear sources and citations
  • Community: Anonymous reporting and neighborhood alerts
  • Innovation: Cutting-edge AI for public service
Important Emergency Notice

For immediate life-threatening emergencies, always call 911 directly.

SafeIndy Assistant is designed to complement, not replace, existing emergency services. While we strive for accuracy, always verify critical information through official channels.
